About Clean&Fly

The Clean&Fly project introduces AI powered drones as garbage collector helpers. Just a one drone can clean various areas in the city. It can pick up cigarettes, garbage, plastic and other waste. The drones can be released in forest or nature areas as well. A camera is used to differentiate between types of garbage. And a specific handle is used to grab an object and put it in a trash can. Let's clean our environment in a smart way!

ADVANTAGES

Available

Autonomous drones will clean streets during day or night on their own.

Scalable

The region of cleaning can be adapted to personal goals. Starting from a small area up to a footbal field.

Flexible

AI based algorithm can differentiate between types of garbage. This allows to spot and clean waste more thoroughly.

Sustainable

The proposed idea helps to maintain our environment clean and effectively utilise waste.
